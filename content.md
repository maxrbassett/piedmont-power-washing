## Current Site Critique (weaknesses & improvement opportunities)
1. **Generic Wix template, dated look.** Footer "© 2024", site title still reads placeholder "mysite" on subpages — looks unmaintained and hurts trust/SEO.
2. **Single-page with broken nav.** HOME / SERVICES / OUR WORK / ABOUT US all link to the same homepage URL (anchor links not real pages). No real structure.
3. **Weak above-the-fold CTA.** Phone/email shown but no prominent "Get a Free Estimate" button or quote form on the homepage hero. Free Estimates is only in the meta description, not surfaced visually.
4. **Gallery underused.** The before/after photos are the strongest sales asset but are buried in a small embedded Wix Pro Gallery with no before/after framing or captions.
5. **No social proof on-page.** Angie's List badge only; no testimonials, star ratings, years-in-business stat, or trust badges surfaced prominently. "12 years experience" and "eco-friendly chemicals" are buried in About copy.
6. **No hours, no service-area map, no licensing/insured statement.**
7. **Broken/placeholder Instagram link** (`instagram.com/wix/`).
8. **Not obviously mobile-optimized; slow heavy Wix payload** (100+ JS chunks).
9. **No clear services breakdown with benefits** — services are three short blurbs with no detail, pricing guidance, or "what's included."
10. **Inconsistent contact email** (info@ displayed vs eric@ in mailto).
